  ---
  A few practical notes for drawing:
  - At 4× scale, a 12×14 squirrel appears 48×56 pixels on screen — plenty of room for   
  detail
  - Keeping a consistent light source direction (upper-left or upper-right) across all  
  sprites will make the scene feel cohesive
  - House walls and roofs as modular pieces (wall, roof, chimney separately) lets you   
  mix and match to get variety without drawing a unique sprite for every house
  combination
  - The wire_segment bulb wire should have a slight downward curve to look like it's    
  hanging naturally
